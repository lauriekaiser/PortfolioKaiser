About Me

I am a frontend developer who is passionate and confident about building software that improves the lives of others. I have a strong desire to continue to learn and grow by collaborating with other talented people to achieve goals.


In 2020 I was chosen to be a Computer Science Champion at the elementary school in which I was employed. As I learned very simple code to implement to the third graders in my classroom, I made a decision to learn more about coding in the years to come. When I fully committed to a career change in 2022, I dove into learning as much as I could to make a successful transition to software development.


I have developed and designed websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Angular while earning a certificate for frontend development from Simplilearn.
